The aim of the game is to help learn your pizza preference based on a series of pizzas pairs.

You will be asked a series of questions on your favourite pizzas over 5 rounds.

  1. After registering you will have to log in with the username and password you just created.
  2. A form with apear, please fill out your details as best as possible.
  3. Once complete you will enter the training round to practice (see below for important buttons and icons).
  4. Once ready you can start the actual Pizza Preference Game of 5 rounds. Within each round you will be shown a series of pizzas pairs and asked which one you would prefer. To help you make your choice you can look at the toppings.
  5. At the end you will be given the option to have a pizza prediction delivered to you via email.

It should take roughly 10 minutes to complete... Enjoy!!

About

The aim of this game is to gather data about users pizza preferances for the purposes of training a machine learning model. This is part of a masters project, the data from this game will be saved anonymously, in a safe way and means no one will be able to identify you after the form has been submitted.
