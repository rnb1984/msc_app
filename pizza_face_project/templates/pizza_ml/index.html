The aim of the experement is to help learn your pizza preference based on a series of pizzas pairs.
You will be asked a series of questions on your favourite pizzas over 5 rounds.

  1. First click Next, at the bottom of this page, to register a username and password. This will prompt you to log in with the same username and password you created.
  2. You will then be presented with a form, please fill in all feilds.
  3. Once completed you will enter the training round to practice (see below for important buttons and icons).
  4. Once ready you can start the actual Pizza Preference Game of 5 rounds. Within each round you will be shown a series of pizzas pairs and asked which one you would prefer. To help you make your choice you can look at the toppings. Please note that if you leave in this mode you will have to restart the game mode if you return, from round 1.
  5. At the end you will be asked if we can use you data from the game and given the option to have a pizza prediction delivered to you via email.

The game mode should roughly take roughly 12 minutes to complete... Enjoy!!

About This Experiment

The aim of this experiment is to gather data about users pizza preferances for the purposes of training a machine learning model.
This is part of a masters project, the data from this experement will be saved anonymously, in a safe way and means no one will be able to identify you after the form has been submitted.
If you could fill in the following form and pick your preferances to the best of your ability.
Once the experement is completed you will be asked if we can email you with your results, then anonymous data will be collected and analysed with other anonymous data.
